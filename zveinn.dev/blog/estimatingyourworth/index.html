What are you worth ?

Estimating your worth as an engineer

Written by Zveinn > sveinn@zkynet.io

In today’s economy it has become increasingly difficult to estimate one’s worth. In This video I will explore how we can estimate our salary/worth using a few basic techniques.

When looking for a new job, you want to (summary):

Analyze your developer level (junior/mid/senior)

Here is Tim Corey talking about how to analyze your skill level

Analyze the market to calculate average salary for your position using the analysis of your developer level

Analyzing the market can be tricky, here is a tool github uses to find average salaries in certain countries and here is a tool called payscale which can also be helpful.

Create a resume that reflects your developer level and experience

Resume.io is a great platform to create a resume and it takes care of all the formatting for you.

Lastly, be aware of people trying to manipulate you into working for free or without a contract.

DO NOT WORK WITHOUT A CONTRACT OR UPFRONT PAYMENT, even if you are new and inexperienced.
